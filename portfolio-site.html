My name is Brittney Jackson. I was born and raised in Connecticut. I like to draw. Normally, I draw digitally on my iPad. I have been doing that for about 2 years now. I've always loved to draw, I just didn't take the time to perfect my skills over the years. I still draw with pencil and paper from time to time but prefer to <draw digitally> because it's so much more versatile. DIY projects are how I relax. It's enjoyable to take something and make it into something completely different.

    Believe it or not, I devoloped my love of computers by just being on MySpace. I would spend most of my nights and weekends when I wasn't in school editing code for layouts. It was fun to see how some words and puncuation could change the whole look of a website. After that I started getting into photo editing and typography. I enjoyed this so much, that I decided to major in Graphic Design in college.
